2. Description of Service

PaperMagic is a digital "paper dolls" application that allows users to:

  • Dress up digital characters with virtual accessories and stickers across 5 categories
  • Create and save custom character designs to a personal gallery
  • Generate printable PDFs for physical paper doll activities (color or outline versions)
  • Access a catalog of characters including humans, animals, and fantasy creatures

The app is designed primarily for children ages 4–12 with parental oversight, with additional support for teens (13–15) and adults (16+).
